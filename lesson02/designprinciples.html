White Space and Clean Design

Mercedes Benz

  • Mercedes Benz Official Website

White space is the blank area around, inside, or between the elements of a design. This site uses white space to create a simple and clean site that is easy and not too complicated to view

PARC: Repetition

The Emirates Group

  • Fly Emirates

In this site we take note of the use of a rectangular shape to relay important information with the help of images within those rectangles and most importantly the repetition principle is witnessed by the use of a red line below the first word in each sub-topic
