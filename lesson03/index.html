Site Name

The site name will be byochamber.com. The abbreviation of 'byo'in the name refers to Bulawayo city and the addition of chamber in the name refers to Bulawayo Chamber of Commerce.

Site Purpose

The main purpose of the site is to be one stop resource for its members in business and those looking for business opportunities in and around the city. The site will also give connect business or those looking to do business in the city to the whole community in helping businesses succeed at the same time improving the livelihood of the citizenry. It will also give a news report of events held in the city and upcoming events, important historic dates, key corporate social initiatives that are in progress throughout the whole city.
